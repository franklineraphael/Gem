    Frequently Asked Questions

    Why do I need to choose a high-resolution image for my hoodie?

    Higher resolution images ensure your photo prints crisp and clear. Low-res images can appear pixelated or blurry on the finished product. We recommend at least 1500×1500 pixels at 300 DPI for best results.

    Can I preview my design before I order?

    Yes! After uploading your photo and entering your slogan, a live preview will generate so you can see exactly how your hoodie will look before placing your order.

    Can I cancel my order after it has been placed?

    Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, production begins and cancellations are no longer possible. Please double-check your design before ordering.

    How do I track my order?

    Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number and a link to follow your package in real time.

    Something before I get my refund?

    For defective or incorrectly printed items, we issue a full refund or replacement within 2 business days of receiving your complaint. Just email us with photos of the issue.
